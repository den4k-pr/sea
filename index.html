FAQ

How does this help me get a leaner body?
It connects the main levers behind body composition: strength training, protein, daily movement, hydration, sleep, and consistency. When these work together, fat loss becomes more realistic and the body looks firmer, not just smaller.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. You’re covered by a 14-day money-back guarantee.
If you’re not happy within 14 days, contact support or DM @zubalenok.team. After the refund form is completed, the refund is processed within 48 hours.
What if I miss a day?
Nothing breaks. The goal is not perfection — it is pattern awareness. One missed day shows you what happened; it does not mean you restart from zero.
Do I need to be fit before starting?
No. You can start from your current level. The lessons are progressive, so your body builds strength step by step. You do not need gym experience, heavy weights, or advanced fitness skills.
Can I repeat one lesson several times?
Yes - and in many cases, it helps.
Repetition gives your body time to understand the movement, improve control, and feel safer in new ranges. If one lesson feels especially useful, challenging, or important for your body, repeat it before moving forward.
What if training usually feels uncomfortable for me?
We don’t want you to force your body. The Zubalenok Method is built around controlled movement, progressive flexibility, strength support, and body awareness. You learn how to move deeper step by step instead of pushing into painful positions.

If something feels too intense, you can reduce the range, slow down, pause, or repeat an easier lesson.
How often should I train?
For best results, train 3–5 times per week.

If you’re a beginner, start with 3 lessons per week. If you already train or stretch regularly, you can do 4–5 lessons per week. The goal is not to do everything perfectly. The goal is to stay consistent and let your body adapt.
Do I need equipment?
You can start with simple home equipment. A mat is recommended. Some lessons may use props like blocks, a chair, or resistance bands to help with support, alignment, or deeper control. You don’t need a gym. Everything is designed for home training.
Can I use this on my phone, TV, or tablet?
Yes. You can access the programs from any device with a browser: phone, tablet, laptop, or smart TV. Many students cast the lessons to their TV and train alongside the video.
Will this help me lose fat?
Yes, the program is designed to support fat loss through the combination that matters most: strength training, daily movement, food awareness, hydration, sleep, and consistency. It is not an extreme diet program — it helps you create the structure your body needs to become leaner over time.
Why are these 30 minute workouts?
Because 30 minutes is realistic enough to repeat and structured enough to work. Each session has a clear sequence: warm-up, strength block, and cool down. The result comes from consistency across 3 months, not from one exhausting workout.

The program mixes slow strength work and faster conditioning-style circuits. Slow lessons help build muscle tone, control, and strength. Faster lessons help improve energy, endurance, and full-body conditioning. Both are included on purpose.
